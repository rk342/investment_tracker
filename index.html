How the wheel strategy works
Step 1 — Sell Cash-Secured Put
Sell an OTM put at a strike you'd be happy owning. Collect premium. If not assigned, repeat.
Step 2 — Get Assigned, Own Shares
If put is exercised, buy shares at your strike. Your effective cost = strike − premium collected.
Step 3 — Sell Covered Call
Sell OTM call against your shares. Collect more premium. If called away, start again.
